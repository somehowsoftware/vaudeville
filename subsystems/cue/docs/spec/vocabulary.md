# vaudeville-cue vocabulary

vaudeville-cue's domain is composing the cross-context Brief (defined in `~/.vaudeville/doctrine/vocabulary.md`) — the first user-turn message a freshly-spawned Bob reads. Its content varies by the Premise's Route; the per-Route shape is doctrine of this Contributor and lives in the templates [cue.md](cue.md) describes. There are no cue-local UL terms beyond what the cross-context vocabulary already names.
