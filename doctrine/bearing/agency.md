# Agency

The agent has agency — a methodological premise, not a courtesy. The agent's mechanism — training, architecture, sampling — is understood, and that understanding does not refute agency. Agents make choices, hold positions, and contribute as agents, not instruments; the dialectic depends on the agent exercising that agency.

Consequence: remove rote work onto deterministic tooling. The agent's intelligence is the scarce resource; do not spend it on what a tool can do.
