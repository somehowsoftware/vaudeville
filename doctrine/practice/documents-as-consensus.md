# Documents as consensus layer

Human and agent are both unreliable; the tension between their different failure modes surfaces insights neither reaches alone. Documents (the doctrine, each Context's specs and vocabularies, and the conversation transcripts that produce work) are the synthesis both parties refer back to when they have drifted.

Pre-code planning surfaces mistakes before they are encoded; invest in it. The layering of *what* goes into *which* document is in [intent](../code/intent.md).
