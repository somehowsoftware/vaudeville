Secondary prose in the sense of the vocabulary's [Doctrine](../doctrine/vocabulary.md#doctrine) entry: read rather than executed, subordinate to the primary; where an essay and the primary conflict, the primary wins and the essay is revised.

- [The engine](the-engine.md) — the depth thesis argued whole: the dyad, the inversion and its boundary, targeted friction, what the evidence says about arguing, and the wager priced honestly.
- [Davar chadash](davar-chadash.md) — the generative axiom argued: the agent decouples what human practice entangled, the evidence for each decoupling, and the lineage the engineering claims.
- [Best practices are answers](practices.md) — practices historicized to the cost structures that asked for them, the honest split between what re-derives and what doesn't, and the re-derivations worked.
- [The miss](the-miss.md) — a comedy of errors in five acts, signed by its principal performer: the composition failure, the experiment it produced, the failure of the experiment's own judge, and the punchline about what working in this environment costs the human.
